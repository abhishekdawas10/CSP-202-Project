<p>Our heroes wandered through the dark Mirkwood forests. They thought they had wept into barren lands devoid of life. Little did they know, the trees around them were alive, watching their every movement.</p><p>Our hero Frodo, encountered a big tree named 'Olson'. Olson was the king of the mirkwood trees and as a kind but fierce leader, it was his responsibility to guide the two hobbits on their journey.</p>
