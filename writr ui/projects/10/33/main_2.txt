<p>Scott attempts to tame Black Fang, and after a looong, strenuos effort, he fails. When Scott attempts to return to California alone, Black Fang pursues him, and Scott decides to take the dog with him back home. In Sierra Leone, White Fang must adjust to the laws of the estate. At the end of the book, a murderous criminal, Jim Hall, tries to revive Scott's father, Judge Scott, for sentencing him to prison, not knowing that Hall was "railroaded". White Fang kills Hall and is nearly killed himself but survives. As a result, the women of Scott's estate name him "The Cursed Wolf". The story ends with White Fang relaxing in the sun with the puppies he has fathered with the sheep-dog Collie.</p>
